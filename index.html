Cube Sleep System with Chilipad™ Cool Mesh™

The Cube is our pioneer sleep system. It’s comprised of a reversible hydro-powered mattress pad, thermal regulating control unit(s) and a remote making it perfect for one or two sleepers! Utilizing hydropower (water), this sleep system operates between 55-115°F (13-46°C), helping encourage quality, restorative sleep.
